Our Insurance Services

Insurance Repairs

We can handle all insurance repairs, including damage caused by fire, water, and storms. Once your insurance claim is accepted, Maximum Build, as the builder, steps in to rectify the damage and restore your property to its original condition.

Expert Damage Assessment

Our team provides thorough damage assessments, ensuring that all aspects of the damage are accounted for, helping to ensure your claim covers the full extent of the repairs needed.

Seamless Insurance Process

We streamline the insurance process, working directly with your insurer to handle paperwork and ensure timely approvals, so you can focus on getting your property back to normal as quickly as possible.
