Why Choose Us?

At Maximum Build, we do not just construct buildings; we create spaces where you make memories and dreams come to life. Your home reflects who you are, and it deserves the very best.

We recognise that major renovations, extensions, or new home constructions are significant decisions. With numerous factors to consider, partnering with a reliable builder who can help you achieve a high-quality outcome is essential.

Maximum Build Craftsmanship Guarantee:

  • 1. Family-Owned Values: As an Australian family-owned business, we prioritise trust, integrity, and craftsmanship, ensuring personalised service and attention throughout your project.
  • 2. Exceptional Craftsmanship: From concept to completion, we build custom homes that match your vision.
  • 3. Comprehensive Services: Our skilled team brings years of experience and a commitment to quality, meticulously crafting every detail to exceed your expectations.
  • 4. Transparency and Integrity: We believe in clear communication and openness throughout the building process. We provide accurate timelines and project updates so you're never left in the dark.
  • 5. Client-Centric Approach: Your ideas and concerns are our priority. We listen carefully to ensure your vision is at the forefront while providing expert guidance every step of the way.
  • 6. Local Expertise: Focusing on the Gold Coast and Brisbane, we leverage our knowledge of local needs and styles to create homes that seamlessly fit your lifestyle.
  • 7. Commitment to Ethical Practices: We are dedicated to restoring faith in the building industry, ensuring your project meets the highest standards without hidden costs.
  • 8. Insured and Licensed: Maximum Build is a fully licensed and insured building company.

Do I need council approval and plans?

For major building work, council approval and certified plans are essential. We can walk you through the entire process and ensure council certification/approval is complete before the commencement of your build.

Can you assist with plans and design?

We have a trusted drafting and design company with whom we have worked for years. They can assist you with making your vision a reality.

How can I book a service?

Booking a service with Maximum Build is simple! Just visit our website, choose the service you need, and fill out the booking form with your details. You can also schedule a consultation by calling our office or emailing us. Once your request is received, our team will contact you to confirm the details and schedule an appointment at your convenience.
